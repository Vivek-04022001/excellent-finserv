Account Deletion Request

Excellent Finserv

At Excellent Finserv, we respect your privacy and give you complete control over your data. If you wish to delete your Excellent Finserv account and associated information, please follow the steps outlined below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • Send an email from your registered email address to support@excellentfinserv.com
  • Mention the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Include the following details in the email:
    • Your full name
    • Your registered mobile number
    • Your User ID / Registration Number
  • Our support team will verify your identity for security purposes.
  • Once verified, your account deletion request will be processed.
  • You will receive a confirmation email after successful deletion.
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All personal data, wallet details, transaction history, and referral information will be deleted, except where retention is required by applicable laws or regulations.
