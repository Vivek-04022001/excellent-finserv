Excellent Finserv – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14-08-2025

Excellent Finserv (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your trust and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Excellent Finserv mobile application and related services, including digital payments, wallet features, and referral-based (MLM) programs.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Date of birth (if required for verification)
  • Profile photo (optional)

b) Identity & Verification Information

  • KYC details (such as PAN, Aadhaar or other government-issued ID, where legally required)
  • Verification status and documents submitted by the user

c) Financial & Payment Information

  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Payment records, recharge history, withdrawals, and transfers
  • Bank account or UPI details (stored securely and only where required)

Note: We do not store complete debit/credit card numbers on our servers.

d) Referral & MLM Network Information

  • Referral codes and referral relationships
  • Downline / upline structure data
  • Commission, rewards, incentives, and earnings history

e) Device & Technical Information

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App version and crash logs (for performance and security)

f) Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used within the app
  • Date, time, and duration of usage
  • Interaction logs for fraud detection and system improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create, verify, and manage user accounts
  • Enable wallet services, payments, withdrawals, and transfers
  • Operate referral and MLM reward systems
  • Process commissions, incentives, and payouts
  • Ensure compliance with legal, tax, and regulatory requirements
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or misuse
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and user experience
  • Send important updates, alerts, and service notifications

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Payment partners and banking service providers (for transaction processing)
  • Cloud and technology service providers (hosting, analytics, security)
  • Regulatory or legal authorities when required by law
  • Internal compliance and audit teams for operational integrity

All third parties are required to maintain strict confidentiality and use your data only for the intended purpose.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data. However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

You may request account deletion, subject to regulatory and legal retention requirements.

6. User Rights

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account (where legally permissible)
  • Withdraw consent for promotional communications

7. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate third-party services such as payment gateways, analytics tools, or login providers. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Excellent Finserv is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
